Overview

Lagori, also known as Seven Stones or Pithoo, is a traditional outdoor game played with a ball and a pile of stones. It is a test of speed, strategy, and coordination.

Popularity Today

Still widely played in rural areas and during local festivals, Lagori remains a beloved pastime for children. Its simple rules and minimal equipment have helped it endure over time.

Tournaments & Competitions

While large-scale tournaments are uncommon, local competitions and cultural events often feature Lagori, celebrating its heritage and encouraging participation.

Future & Challenges

With urbanization and modern sports gaining prominence, traditional games like Lagori face the challenge of staying relevant. Community programs and school events are crucial for its revival.
